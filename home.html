Design the four-seasons.html page, using internal CSS as required.

Four Seasons (boxes)

AUTUMN

When autumn comes, the gentle warmth of autumn will gradually replace the sweltering summer sun. The coming of autumn is not only a transformation of nature but it also brings with it many changes of the human soul.

A new look — a new emotion and possibly a new beginning.

WINTER

Winter is the season suitable for late love. Season of mistakes and worries. The season when myrtle stands hard in the cold rain but cannot bloom.

Spring begins with the cold to numb from the end of winter.

SPRING

Spring is the season when life returns and flowers awaken. Spring brings the earth back to vivid color and new growth.

A time for renewal and hope.

SUMMER

Summer rain comes and goes suddenly bringing cool air to ease the burning summer sun. Memories flood back; the first rain of the season brings bountiful crops.

Hot days and bright skies.
